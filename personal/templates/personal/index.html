About

Code Beta is an competitive coding competition organized by LNCT CSE Department for the Tech Fest PLANET ENGINEER'S 2018. Recent years have proved to be the epitome to the rise of a new phase in IT industry where naive skills are not enough to survive in the market.

This event is organized to provide the students with a platform to enhance their coding skills.

It's time for all the coders to feel the excitement. Code Beta, a coding competition is a delightful playground for the exploration of intelligence of the programmers by analyzing real-life problems and using those skills to solve them. It is designed to test your programming skills and logical thinking.

The competition will consist of three rounds:-

  • 1 . MCQ Round
  • 2.Coding Round – I (Easy)
  • 3.Coding Round – II (Medium and Hard)

Rules

There is a set of rules and a code of conduct that we beleive that you should adhere to in exam and behaviour
  • 1. All contestants must have registered by 23rd March, 2018.

  • 2. Contestants must be present on the forementioned venue on time (i.e. 9:00 am) or they will be subject to immediate disqualification.

  • 3. During the event, if a team or an individual is caught using any unethical means while attempting a question, that team or individual will be immediately disqualified from the competition.

  • 4. For MCQ Round, the result of the online judge will be final and subject to no protest.

  • 5. For Coding Rounds, the result of the online judge will be final and subject to no change
